Who We Are

The story behind EduFinder and Waystar Learning.

Who we are

EduFinder, built by Waystar Learning.

EduFinder is an online platform for education. Waystar Learning launched it in 2026.

Every day, we work hard so that EduFinder can give students and parents around the world the best quality of information.

Where we are

Rooted in Seoul, made for international study.

Our company is based in Seoul, South Korea. We focus on families who are aiming for a quality international education.

Our goal is to equip them with localized and less-known information, so that their time in schools and academies can be fully enjoyable as well as productive. In the near future, we hope this insightful experience can be translated into invaluable services for the rest of the world too.

How we build

Powered by big data and frontier AI.

Many of us do not hold a computer science degree. Even so, everyone at Waystar believes in the power of the latest technological breakthroughs, especially big data and AI.

That belief is why we have shipped several features that lean on sophisticated algorithms and frontier AI models. They are built to empower students to ace the tests they need to take, and to present their truest potential to prestigious institutions.

Who it is for

For students and the families behind them.

Big decisions in education are rarely made alone. A student picks a path, but parents, siblings, mentors, and friends all help carry it.

EduFinder is built for that whole circle. We try to make every tool useful whether you are the one studying, the one paying tuition, or the one cheering from the sidelines.

What is next

The journey keeps going.

Our journey to make every service inside EduFinder more useful and more accessible will continue.

We will keep listening to families, refining what already works, and adding what helps. There is still a lot to build.
